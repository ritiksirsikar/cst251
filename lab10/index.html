Lab 10 - Styling Text Elements

Challenges

The challenges for this lab are:

  1. Surrounding the page with div id = "contents" and putting divs around the section.
  2. Select a new font for your body text. Good web design suggests us to keep plain and readable.
  3. Style your headings with color, text-transform, and letter-spacing

Problems

The problems I was facing was to come up with some new style and understanding what suits best for the h1's and h2's. I was trying to understand how much does a heading text matters and how it draws attention to the website. I was searching for the best text that can look easy to read and will draw attention. I also learned how to use text-transform and letter spacing feature.

Results

I created a webpage that will draw attention if developed more. I also made the font easy to read and something which does not look boring. I also learned the display gradient and how to use it to make the webpage look more premium.
